About

Join the pack of Clydesdales (and one special donkey!) on a trip to remember...
Experience the horse life on the croft, in an idyllic location 7 miles north of Ullapool on the West Coast of Scotland, making us a great stop on your North Coast 500 trip!

*More to be added*

Visit

On arrival you will be introduced to your Clydesdale and after a talk on safety, and how to lead your Clydesdale, we will set off on a 30-40-minute walk, taking in the picturesque highland scenery.
You will walk with your Clydesdale (not ride) on a flat, single track private road and there will be lots of time to stop and take photos of you with your Clydesdale in the beautiful surroundings.
After the walk, we will take the Clydesdales home to the croft, where you will be taught how to groom and look after these gentle giants and experience them up-close and personal.
Overall the experience will be approximately 90 minutes.
